Banana Bread

Why compromise the banana favour? This banana bread is moist and delicious with loads of banana flavour!

Banana bread that's moist, has lots of flavour and is dense. What's not to love?

This banana bread is also great toasted!

  5. Yield: 1 loaf

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (400g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon (4.2g) baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon (1.4g) salt
  • 1/2 cup (200g) butter
  • 3/4 cup (300g) brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups (475g) mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out into a wire rack.
